# Pizza App

Welcome to 9jaPizza, your go-to pizza delivery app for satisfying all your cravings! Below you'll find everything you need to know to get started with our app.

Our Specially made unique pizza consists of various recipes and quality ingredients tailored to give you a mind blowing experience at the first bite.

- Exploring the Menu:
  You can browse through our delicious menu section to select the pizza you love from our variety of pizzas.

- Placing an Order:
  Once you've made your selection, proceed to the submit order.
  you will be redirected to a checkout page where you will asked to input your details.

- Tracking Your Order:
  Sit back and relax while we prepare your freshly made pizza, a time frame of the preparation will be shown on your order slip which will also include your order ID.

- Enjoying Your Pizza:
  Once your order arrives, dig in and enjoy your piping hot pizza!
  Don't forget to make your payment once the order arrives at your doorstep.
